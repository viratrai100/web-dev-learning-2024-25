Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • Flour
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Sugar
  • Baking Soda
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and baking soda to form a dough.
  2. Shape the dough into small round balls.
  3. Fry the balls in ghee until golden brown.
  4. Soak the fried balls in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashews
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Make sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water.
  3. Add cashew powder to the syrup and cook until it thickens.
  4. Pour the mixture onto a plate, flatten it, and let it cool. Cut into diamond-shaped pieces.

Jalebi

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, yeast, and water to make a batter and let it rise.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and pipe the batter into the oil in a spiral shape.
  3. Fry until golden brown and then dip in warm sugar syrup flavored with cardamom.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy the crispy, sweet jalebis!

Motichur Laddu

Ingredients

  • Besan (Chickpea Flour)
  • Sugar
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Cashews and Raisins

Recipe

  1. Roast besan in ghee until it turns golden brown.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and water to make a thick mixture.
  3. Mix in roasted cashews and raisins, then shape the mixture into small round laddus.
  4. Let them cool and serve.
